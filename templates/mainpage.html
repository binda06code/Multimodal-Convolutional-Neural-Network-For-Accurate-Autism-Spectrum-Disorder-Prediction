How does Multimodal work?

  • Upload Facial Images

    Submit PNG, JPG, or JPEG files of facial images. Our system analyzes facial features related to Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD).

  • Upload Eye Tracking Data

    Provide CSV files containing eye tracking data. Our technology evaluates eye patterns associated with ASD.

  • Predict ASD

    Combine uploaded data for a comprehensive ASD prediction. Get actionable insights quickly, whether using facial images, eye tracking data, or both.
